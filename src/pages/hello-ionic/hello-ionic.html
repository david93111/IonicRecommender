IA Game Recommender

Welcome to the Spark Game Recommender

This is a project based on microservices architecture, through the use of spark MlLib for machine learning this app generates recomendations, focused on colaborative filtering

This is the front end, developed on Ionic3 that allows you to comunicate whit the MicroService developed in Scala and Akka That is incharge of train de ALS model and recommend the games.

For now you can only see games recommended to you based on ALS Colaborative Filtering Algorithm, but soon you will have the functionalities to rate a game, like a game, and have recommendations based on similar people based on your favorite selected categories and your owned consoles.
Stay tuned for all the soon improvents!
